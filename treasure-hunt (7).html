🔧 Upload Troubleshoot

Video not uploading? Try these steps.

1
Check File Size

Video files above 50MB may fail. Try trimming or compressing the video in your gallery before uploading.

2
Check Your Connection

Switch to a stable WiFi connection. Mobile data uploads can time out for large video files.

3
Try a Different Format

If your video is in MOV format (iPhone), it may be large. Try an MP4 video from your gallery instead.

4
Reload and Retry

Close this page, open it again by scanning the QR code, and try uploading once more.

🆘 Still not working?

If none of the steps above help, you can use the Alternate Submission option below — solve a riddle instead to complete this checkpoint.
